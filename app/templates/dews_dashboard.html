🔐 Layer 1 — Watermark Injector
HMAC-SHA256 + Steganographic Salt
Every worker update is cryptographically signed with an HMAC-SHA256 tag binding worker identity to the exact payload. Simultaneously a deterministic steganographic salt is embedded at the 4th decimal place of the quantity field.

A worker copying another worker's data is caught because the salt won't match their identity.
salt = (worker_id × 7919) mod 9 × 0.0001
🔍 Layer 2 — IDS Pipeline
4-Node Inspection via Node-RED
Every submission passes through four inspection nodes:
Node 1 Verify HMAC tag validity
Node 2 Verify steganographic salt
Node 3 Impossible delta check (hours ≤ 18)
Node 4 Route: PASS → ELIS / BREACH → Alert
